Living in California

I only lived in Long Beach, California for a brief four months but what I experienced there was enough for me to want to visit again. I didn't get to roam the city and experience a lot of it, apart from running around town to get my car registered. I did not get to try the famous In-N-Out Burger, but the few places I did get to try were pretty good. As I said, I didn't have too much time in Long Beach and most of it was trying to find a job. So, instead of places to go it's going to be places I wanted to go to.

The Food

  • This one shocked me a little bit, but Vegan Castle was a new one for me and it was amazing!
  • Hands down from what I've tried, best tacos were at Pepe's Downey. I know there are some other amazing Mexican resturants, but this was one that I got to try.

The Places

  • Joshua Tree is on my list of places to visit.
  • Of course I would have to visit the Hollywood Sign because I feel like it's must once at least.
  • Also, I know there is way more than what I'm listing to see in California, but Santa Monica Pier is going on my list.
